PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Effective immediately

The short version

Maths Jumper does not collect your name, email address, location, or any personal information. All game progress is saved locally on your device only. We do not serve ads, track you across apps, or sell any data. Ever.

📖

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Maths Jumper ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our mobile application. Maths Jumper is a children's educational game designed to help players practise their times tables.

We are committed to protecting the privacy of all players, especially children. This policy complies with the Google Play Developer Policy, the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the UK Children's Code (Age Appropriate Design Code), and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

By downloading or playing Maths Jumper, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

🗂️

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Maths Jumper is designed to work entirely offline and stores all information locally on your device.

The following data is saved only on your device using your browser's or device's local storage, and is never transmitted to us or any third party:

  • Game progress (stages completed, questions answered)
  • Stars and coins earned
  • Per-fact accuracy and mastery records
  • Selected character and cosmetic settings
  • Audio and display preferences
This data exists solely on your device. Uninstalling the app or clearing app data will permanently remove it. We have no access to it.

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or any account information
  • Location data (precise or approximate)
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, GAID, IMEI, or similar)
  • IP addresses or network information
  • Photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Contacts or calendar data
  • Crash logs or analytics events
👶

3. Children's Privacy

Maths Jumper is directed at children under the age of 13 and is designed to be safe for use by children of all ages. We take children's privacy extremely seriously.

  • No personal data is collected from any user, including children under 13.
  • No parental consent is required because no personal data is ever collected.
  • The App contains no advertising of any kind.
  • The App contains no in-app purchases.
  • The App contains no social features, chat, or user-generated content.
  • The App contains no links to external websites accessible during normal play.
  • The App does not require or request an internet connection to function.

This App fully complies with COPPA and is suitable for use by children without parental supervision from a data-privacy standpoint.

🌐

4. Internet Access & Third Parties

Maths Jumper loads game assets (images, sounds, and video files) from a GitHub repository (github.com/jcoles8472/Maths-Jumper). This is a read-only content delivery mechanism.

When assets are fetched from GitHub, GitHub's own servers handle that request. GitHub may log standard server request data (such as your IP address) in accordance with their own Privacy Policy at docs.github.com. We have no control over and no access to any such data.

We do not use, integrate, or share data with any of the following:

  • Advertising networks or ad SDKs
  • Analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Social media platforms
  • Crash reporting services
  • Payment processors
  • Any other third-party SDK

We use Google Fonts (the Nunito typeface) to render text within the App. When fonts are loaded, Google's servers may process a standard font request. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

🔒

5. Data Storage & Security

All game data is stored locally on the user's device using the device's built-in storage mechanisms. This data:

  • Never leaves the device through our App
  • Is not encrypted by us (it contains no sensitive personal information)
  • Can be deleted at any time by clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App
  • Is not backed up to any cloud service by our App (though a user's device operating system may back up app data as part of standard device backups)

Because we collect no personal data, there is no personal data for us to protect, breach, or lose.

📱

6. App Permissions

Maths Jumper requests only the permissions necessary for gameplay. Below is a full account of permissions used:

  • Internet access — required to load game assets (images, sounds, videos) from our GitHub repository. No personal data is transmitted.
  • Vibration — used to provide optional haptic feedback on button presses. No data is collected.

We do not request permissions for:

  • Camera or microphone
  • Location (GPS or network-based)
  • Contacts or calendar
  • Phone state or call logs
  • Storage read/write beyond app-specific local storage
🚫

7. Advertising

Maths Jumper contains no advertising of any kind. We do not:

  • Display banner, interstitial, or reward ads
  • Use advertising SDKs
  • Collect advertising identifiers (IDFA, GAID)
  • Engage in behavioural or interest-based advertising
  • Sell advertising inventory to any third party
The App's Google Play listing is marked as containing Ads: No and In-app purchases: No.

8. Your Rights

Under applicable privacy law (including GDPR and the UK Data Protection Act 2018), individuals have rights regarding their personal data. Because we hold no personal data about you, most of these rights are satisfied by default.

However, you have the right to:

  • Delete your game data at any time by clearing the App's storage or uninstalling it from your device.
  • Contact us at any time to ask questions about our data practices.
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority if you believe your rights have been infringed. In the UK, this is the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

Residents of California may also have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). As we collect no personal information, we do not sell personal information, and no opt-out is required.
